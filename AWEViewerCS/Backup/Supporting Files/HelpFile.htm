Using the Report Generator Form
The Report Generator form is used to specify criteria for Employees data.

You must select one of the radio buttons in the first column of the table and then fill in the rest of the criteria for the option that you have chosen.

Sick Leave
Select either "less than or equal to" (<=) or "greater than or equal to" (>=) from the drop down list in the middle column.
Use the arrows on the numeric box in the third column to select the value you would like the results to be compared against.

Shift
Select one of the shifts from the list in the third column of the table.

Department
Select one or more departments by clicking the check boxes in the list in the third column of the table.

Hire Date
Select either "less than or equal to" (<=) or "greater than or equal to" (>=) from the drop down list in the middle column.
Use the arrows at the top of the Calendar in the third column of the table to select the year and month of the hire date that you require.
Click on the day that you want your results to be compared against in the Calendar.
Tip - the hire dates range from 31st July 1996 to 2nd January 1999.
